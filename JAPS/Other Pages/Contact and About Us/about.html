Our Story

It all started in 1964 when brothers Florencio, Florentino, and Florinio Santiago established a business called Santiago Shoe Supply shop in Marikina which supplied leather and other shoe materials to other factories.

Business was very good, so Florinio and Florencio decided to have their own business venture and started their own shoe factory businesses while Florentino was left with the shoe supply shop. Florinio established F. Santiago Shoes in 1968 and because of his chinky eyes, he was always referred to as Hapon or Japs, short for Japanese. This was the reason why F. Santiago Shoes was later on known as Japs Shoes.

This was the time wherein the shoe business in Marikina was booming. He supplied shoes in SM, Rustan's, Robinsons, and other big companies.
